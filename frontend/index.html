Instructions for Insect Overflow

Welcome to Insect Overflow. Try your best to survive and have fun!

  • The poison of this land causes you to starve.
  • To stay alive, you must keep feeding.
  • Use the ← ↑ ↓ → (arrow keys) or W A S D keys to move around.
  • Stay away from the poison wall. This will be easy as you can move over your own body.
  • Different foods will give you different effects. (not all of them are desirable)
  • The "yellow bug" regenerates one segment of your body.
  • The "red bug" regenerates more than one segments of your body.
  • The "caterpillar" lends immunity from the poison.
  • The "butterfly" has an unusual effect of increasing your length. It is the only creature known to be able surpass the poison wall entirely.
